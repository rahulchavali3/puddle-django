Privacy Policy

We believe privacy should be simple and easy to understand.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Puddle. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our marketplace platform.

By using Puddle, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Puddle, we may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • • Your username and account information.
  • • Your email address.
  • • Information about items you list for sale.
  • • Messages and information you submit through our contact forms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and improve Puddle, including to:

  • • Create and manage user accounts.
  • • Allow users to list and discover items.
  • • Enable communication between users.
  • • Respond to questions and support requests.
  • • Improve the security and functionality of the platform.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate the platform, provide requested services, comply with legal obligations, or protect the safety and security of Puddle and its users.

5. User-Provided Content

When you create listings or communicate with other users, the information you choose to share may be visible to other users of the platform.

Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in public listings or messages.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information stored on Puddle. However, no online service can guarantee complete security of information transmitted or stored online.

7. Cookies and Sessions

Puddle may use session data and similar technologies to keep users authenticated and provide core functionality of the platform.

8. Your Information

You may contact us if you have questions about the personal information associated with your account or if you need assistance with your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised update date.

Questions?

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us.
